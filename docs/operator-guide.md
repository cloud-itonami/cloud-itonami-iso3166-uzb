# Operator guide — UZB

Portal: `xarid.uzex.uz` (live, independently confirmed this iteration --
own `<title>` tag read directly: "DAVLAT XARIDLARI MAXSUS AXBOROT
PORTALI" / "State/Public Procurement Special Information Portal").
Regulated under Decree of the President of the Republic of Uzbekistan
No. PP-4544 (5 December 2019). This iteration could not independently
confirm or rule out a separate parliamentary "Law on Public Procurement"
distinct from this decree, nor a single named procurement regulator --
both honestly disclosed gaps.

Entity: STIR tax record (State Tax Committee, `soliq.uz`) + business
registration record (`birdarcha.uz`, hyperlinked from gov.uz's own
official page; the specific administering ministry/agency was not
independently confirmed this session). Engagements involving foreign
investment additionally reference the Ministry of Investment, Industry
and Trade (Investment Promotion Agency, `invest.gov.uz`) under the Law
"On investments and investment activity" No. LRU-598.

Engagements declaring locally-engaged staff under an initial probation
clause also clear the Labour Code of the Republic of Uzbekistan's own
Article 130 statutory ceiling gate (3 months for general employees, 6
months for heads of organizations and their deputies, chief accountants,
and heads of separate divisions of organizations) before a filing can
proceed. Human-gated filing only.
